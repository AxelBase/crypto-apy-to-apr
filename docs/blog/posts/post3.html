How to Use This APY ↔ APR Converter

Published: December 19, 2025

This converter provides a simple yet powerful way to translate between APR and APY for any compounding frequency, helping you understand real crypto yields.

Start by entering the rate you want to convert. This could be an APR quoted by a staking platform or an APY advertised by a DeFi protocol.

Next, select the compounding frequency. Daily is the default and most common in crypto staking. Many networks like Ethereum, Cardano, and Solana use daily or near-daily compounding.

Choose your conversion direction. Select APR to APY to see how compounding boosts the effective yield. Choose APY to APR to reveal the base simple rate behind an advertised yield.

Click Convert to see instant results. The tool displays the converted rate to four decimal places for precision.

Practical Examples

The result includes context specific to your conversion. Converting APR to APY explains how staking rewards compound to increase returns. Converting APY to APR shows the underlying simple rate for comparison.

Use different frequencies to compare scenarios. See how monthly versus daily compounding affects the same APR. Continuous compounding provides the theoretical maximum yield.

The tool handles common crypto use cases perfectly. Compare Binance Earn APY against a raw staking APR. Evaluate DeFi lending rates across protocols quoting different metrics.

Examples in action:

  • A lending platform advertises 8% APY daily compounded. Convert to APR to compare with simple interest options.
  • A staking pool offers 12% APR. Convert to daily APY to see real annual return.
  • Compare quarterly compounded savings with daily crypto yields.

Edge cases are handled gracefully. Zero rates return zero. Negative inputs trigger warnings. Extremely high rates (over 1000%) are flagged as potentially inaccurate.

The converter works entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device, ensuring privacy.

Quick Tips

  • Always verify the actual compounding frequency used by the protocol
  • Higher frequency means higher APY for the same APR
  • Use continuous compounding as an upper bound estimate
  • Round-trip conversions should return very close to original

Bookmark the tool for regular use when researching opportunities. Crypto yields change frequently, and accurate comparison requires proper APR/APY translation.

Combine with other research. Check lock-up periods, risks, tokenomics, and historical performance alongside yield metrics.
