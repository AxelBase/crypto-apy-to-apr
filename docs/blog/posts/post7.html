Real-World Examples from Top Staking Platforms

Real-World Examples from Top Staking Platforms

Published: December 19, 2025

Leading cryptocurrency networks and platforms provide concrete examples of how APR and APY work in practice across different staking models.

Ethereum staking through validators typically advertises around 4 to 6 percent annual rewards. These rewards compound daily as new blocks are produced and added to validator balances.

A 5 percent APR on Ethereum becomes approximately 5.13 percent APY due to daily compounding. Liquid staking providers like Lido or Rocket Pool often show this effective APY directly.

Solana staking rewards vary based on network inflation and validator performance. Current rates hover around 6 to 8 percent. Rewards compound every epoch, roughly every two days.

Cardano uses a different model with epoch-based rewards every five days. A typical 5 percent APR translates to about 5.01 percent APY given this frequency.

Centralized Platform Examples

Binance Earn and similar centralized services often display APY prominently. Their flexible staking products might show 8 to 12 percent APY on major assets.

These platforms usually compound rewards daily or weekly. The advertised APY already includes compounding effects, making direct comparison with raw APR quotes essential.

Kraken staking services provide clear breakdowns. They often show both the base reward rate and effective APY after compounding.

Cosmos ecosystem tokens frequently offer double-digit staking rewards. Rates of 15 to 20 percent APR are common, translating to significantly higher APY with frequent compounding.

Polkadot parachain auctions and nominated staking produce variable rewards. Successful nominators typically earn 12 to 18 percent annually with daily or era-based compounding.

Comparison Insights

  • Ethereum: 5% APR → ~5.13% daily APY
  • Solana: 7% APR → ~7.25% near-daily APY
  • Cardano: 5% APR → ~5.01% epoch APY
  • Binance Earn: Often quotes direct APY with daily compounding
  • Cosmos: 18% APR → ~19.72% daily APY
